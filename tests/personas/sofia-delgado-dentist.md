## Daily Tasks
1. Check appointment schedule for the day (who's coming, what procedure)
2. Respond to WhatsApp inquiries about pricing and availability
3. Confirm tomorrow's appointments via WhatsApp reminders
4. Process payments after each appointment
5. Handle emergency appointment requests ("me duele mucho la muela")
6. Update patient treatment plans and notes
7. Check supplies inventory (dental materials, gloves, anesthesia)
8. Respond to post-procedure questions from patients

## Weekly Tasks
1. Review weekly revenue and appointment statistics
2. Follow up with patients who missed appointments (no-shows)
3. Send treatment plan reminders to patients with pending procedures
4. Order dental supplies from distributor
5. Review and approve social media posts (before/after photos)
6. Reconcile Yape/Plin/bank payments with appointments
7. Plan next week's schedule (block time for complex procedures)

## Monthly Tasks
1. SUNAT monthly IGV declaration
2. Pay rent, utilities, staff salaries
3. Review monthly P&L
4. Insurance claim submissions
5. Equipment maintenance scheduling
6. Patient satisfaction follow-ups
7. Marketing: send promotions (teeth whitening specials, etc.)

## Pain Points
- **No-shows kill revenue:** 15-20% no-show rate, each empty slot costs S/150-400
- **Appointment chaos:** Receptionist manages everything in a physical notebook + WhatsApp
- **Payment tracking nightmare:** Mix of Yape, cash, card, Plin — hard to reconcile
- **Patient follow-up:** Forgets to remind patients about their next cleaning or pending treatment
- **Pricing questions all day:** "Cuánto cuesta un blanqueamiento?" repeated 10x daily
- **Emergency scheduling:** Squeezing in urgent cases without double-booking
- **Supply stockouts:** Runs out of materials mid-procedure occasionally
