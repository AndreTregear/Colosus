### 1.2 SMB Onboarding & Activation Playbook (strategy/15)

Transforms Yaya's biggest execution risk — whether semi-literate business owners will complete WhatsApp onboarding — from an unknown into a testable system. Key findings:

- **Target metric:** 60% 7-day activation rate (user completes onboarding + performs one value-generating action within 7 days)
- **"First Value in 5 Minutes" principle:** User must experience Yaya's value BEFORE registration. Not "What's your business name?" but "Want me to remind your next client? Send me their number."
- **5-Phase onboarding flow:** Pre-Onboarding (referral/QR) → First Contact (tap-only, 5 min) → First Value (immediate useful action) → Habit Formation (Days 1-7, progressive disclosure) → Feature Expansion (Weeks 2-4) → Conversion (Month 1+)
- **Voice is non-negotiable for MVP:** 73.4% of users have secondary education or below; voice note processing must be Day 1 infrastructure, not Phase 2
- **Intermediary model validated:** TymeBank (South Africa) achieved 85% onboarding via assisted setup; Yaya should replicate with accountants and beauty supply distributors as "installation technicians"
- **Gamification boosts completion 28%** (Landbot): Badge system, weekly summaries, social proof
- **Anti-patterns:** Don't require account creation before value; don't send feature lists; don't use business jargon; design for 30-second interaction bursts between customers
- **A/B testing roadmap:** 7 priority tests identified (welcome length, first action type, voice response matching, referrer mention, daily messages, badges, paywall ceiling)

**Strategic implication:** Activation is the moat. The product that achieves highest activation wins — not the product with most features. Voice-first, value-before-registration, intermediary-assisted onboarding is the playbook.

- **Category evolution:** Gen 1 (Desktop ERP: Tally/Alegra) → Gen 2 (Mobile app: Vyapar) → **Gen 3 (Conversational AI: Yaya)**
- **Meta's July 2025 WhatsApp voice API:** In-thread voice calling, programmable voice notes, AI voice agent support — all now available via Business API
- **WhatsApp voice stats:** 7B voice messages/day, 100B+ minutes/month of calls
- **100x ARPU advantage:** Yaya's $156/year vs. Vyapar's $1.55/year. Yaya needs 100x fewer users for equivalent revenue. 5,000 paying users = $780K ARR.
- **Annual pricing consideration:** Vyapar's annual model (₹999-1,999/year) drives 100% YoY growth. Yaya should offer S/399/year (~S/33/month effective) to reduce perceived cost below INEI's S/30 mass-adoption ceiling

**Strategic implication:** The category is proven ($180M valuation). The technology is ready (WhatsApp voice API). The ARPU advantage is massive (100x). Yaya doesn't need millions of users — it needs thousands of paying users to build a significant business. Execute fast.

### 2.2 The Activation-to-Partner Pipeline

Connecting strategy/15 (onboarding) + competitive/08 (Vyapar partners) + strategy/09 (salon playbook):

The onboarding playbook reveals that **assisted onboarding** dramatically outperforms self-service in low-literacy markets. Vyapar's 27,000 partners prove this scales. The synthesis:

1. **Phase 1 (Months 1-6):** Andre IS the partner. Customer Zero + first 50 salons onboarded personally
2. **Phase 2 (Months 6-12):** First 10 accountant partners trained as "Yaya installers." Each onboards 5-10 businesses
3. **Phase 3 (Year 2+):** Partner program scales to 50-100 partners. Each partner earns S/200-500/month from referral commissions
4. **Phase 4 (Year 3+):** Top partners earn S/1,000-2,000/month (comparable to Vyapar's top partner earnings adjusted for Peru)

This is the growth engine. Not ads, not viral marketing — trusted humans introducing Yaya to businesses they already serve.

### 2.3 The Voice-Compliance-Finance Stack

Connecting competitive/08 (WhatsApp voice) + technology/10 (SIRE) + strategy/15 (activation):

The three documents reveal a coherent product sequence driven by voice:

1. **Voice → Activation:** User sends voice note → Yaya performs useful action (reminder, sale log). This is the 5-minute activation
2. **Voice → Compliance:** User dictates invoice by voice → Yaya generates SUNAT-compliant CPE → Sends to SIRE. This is the killer feature
3. **Voice → Finance:** Invoices accumulate → Transaction history builds → Credit scoring becomes possible → Factoring/lending referrals unlock embedded finance

**The insight:** Voice isn't just a UX choice — it's the entry point for the entire value chain. Without voice, the activation rate drops, the compliance feature loses its simplicity advantage, and the finance stack never gets the data it needs.
