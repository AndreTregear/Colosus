## 1. New Research Added This Cycle

### 1.1 Peru's Digital Payments Revolution & Open Finance (fintech/03)

Complete mapping of Peru's payment infrastructure and its implications for Yaya. Key findings:

- **Yape dominates with 16M+ active users** — roughly 2/3 of all Peruvian adults. Plin adds 10M+ more. Combined: 28M accounts, 183M+ monthly interoperable transactions
- **591 digital operations per capita** annually (BCRP H1 2025) — Peruvians average 1.6 digital transactions per day. The behavioral shift from cash is irreversible
- **QR interoperability advancing:** BCRP mandating unified QR (one code for Yape, Plin, and all bank apps). Phase 4 (Payment Initiation) will allow third-party platforms to trigger payments via standardized APIs
- **SBS published Open Finance roadmap (February 26, 2026):** 4 phases from Diagnosis (2026) through Open Banking (2027-2029) to full Open Finance (2029+). User consent-first model following Brazil's precedent
- **Critical SMB pain point identified:** "Pantallazos falsos" (fake payment screenshots) and manual Yape/Plin reconciliation. 0% commission means everyone accepts digital payments, but nobody tracks them properly
- **New entrants:** Revolut, BTG Pactual, Prex, Bipay, Kori all applying for Peru licenses. Multi-modal licensing (e-money + lending in one license) expected H1 2026
- **30% of adults have zero financial products; only 34-35% have credit.** Open Finance explicitly designed to close this gap

### 1.2 WhatsApp Business API 2026: Pricing & Policy Deep Dive (strategy/10)

Definitive analysis of the two major 2025-2026 WhatsApp platform changes. Key findings:

- **Per-message pricing (July 2025) dramatically reduces Yaya's costs.** Service messages (customer-initiated, ~60-70% of Yaya's traffic) are completely free. Utility messages within service windows are also free. LATAM pricing is 5-10× cheaper than Europe
- **Revised WhatsApp COGS: $0.10-0.68/user/month** (down from $0.52-3.02). This is a 65-80% reduction in WhatsApp costs
- **Total COGS per user drops to $0.90-1.48/month** (from $1.32-3.82). Gross margins at S/49 pricing: **89-93%**
- **The 72-hour CTWA hack:** Users acquired via Click-to-WhatsApp ads generate zero messaging fees for 72 hours. Entire onboarding flow (7 messages) costs $0 in WhatsApp fees
- **AI policy ban (January 15, 2026) explicitly protects Yaya.** Meta banned general-purpose AI assistants (ChatGPT, Perplexity) but explicitly allows task-specific business bots (appointment management, invoicing, customer support). Yaya is fully compliant
- **Italy and EU challenging the ban** — AGCM ordered Meta to suspend; EU Commission investigating. Even if ban holds, Yaya unaffected. If ban is relaxed, more competitors could emerge
- **Watch H2 2026:** WhatsApp usernames (searchable businesses), BSUIDs (anonymized user IDs), potential LATAM local currency billing
- **Only 11-12 paying users needed to cover $130/month infrastructure** at updated COGS

- Overhead: $1,630/month (infra + Andre)
- Breakeven: $1,630 / $11.81 = **138 paying users** (1,380 total at 10% conversion)

This validates the seedstrapping model: Andre can prove unit economics with <150 paying users before hiring, then use the seedstrap raise to scale the team.
