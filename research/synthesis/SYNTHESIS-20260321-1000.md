## 1. New Research Added This Cycle

### 1.1 Peruvian Spanish Voice AI & Localization (technology/09)

Complete technical specification for Yaya's voice and language capabilities. Key findings:

- **Five distinct Peruvian Spanish dialects** require phased support: Coastal (Phase 1, well-served by standard models), Andean-Coastal (Phase 2, majority of Lima's periphery), Andean/Amazonic/Equatorial (Phase 3+)
- **62% of WhatsApp daily users send voice notes** globally; in LATAM, Argentina leads at 29.5 hours/month on WhatsApp. Voice note processing is not optional — it's core functionality
- **Whisper Large V3 Turbo** is the recommended STT model: 99+ languages, 6x faster than standard, deployable on c.yaya.sh (2× RTX A5000). Estimated latency: <2 seconds for 30-second voice note
- **Peruvian business slang (jerga)** must be handled natively: chamba (work), plata (money), misio (broke), pata/causa (friend), yapa (free extra), "pe/pue" (uniquely Peruvian pues truncation)
- **Cultural communication patterns** mapped: diminutive-heavy speech, "ya" as multipurpose word, usted-default in business, present perfect for recent past
- **Three-phase implementation:** Month 1-6 (Whisper Turbo + Google Cloud TTS es-PE), Month 6-12 (fine-tune on collected data), Month 12+ (local XTTS with Peruvian voice model)
