### 2.2 Whisper Noise Robustness & Salon Deployment Guide (technology/14)

**Purpose:** Evidence-based technical guide for deploying Whisper in a noisy salon environment.

**Headline finding: Do NOT apply denoising to audio before Whisper.**

A December 2025 paper ("When De-noising Hurts," arXiv 2512.17562) systematically tested speech enhancement on four ASR systems including Whisper across 40 configurations. Result: **denoising degraded performance in every single case**, with WER increases of 1.1% to 46.6%. At the SNR most relevant to salons (10 dB), denoised audio had 25.83% WER vs. 8.82% for raw noisy audio — a catastrophic 17 percentage point degradation.

**Why:** Whisper was trained on 680,000 hours of real-world noisy audio and has learned internal noise robustness. External denoising removes acoustic features that Whisper uses for recognition.

**Additional findings:**
- A March 19, 2026 Cambridge study shows Whisper large-v3 **outperforms human listeners** in all noise conditions except pub noise
- Expected salon environment accuracy: **88-94% WER** for financial utterances at typical noise levels (15-20 dB SNR)
- The `initial_prompt` parameter with domain-specific vocabulary provides **5-10% WER improvement for free**
- `faster-whisper` (CTranslate2-based) recommended over standard Whisper: **4x faster, lower VRAM**
- End-to-end latency budget: **1,500-2,800ms** (within 3-second target)
- Whisper's error pattern in noise: confidently produces wrong words rather than "[inaudible]" — makes confirmation Flow critical

**Optimal configuration provided:** Docker deployment, FastAPI server, initial_prompt with Peruvian salon vocabulary, faster-whisper setup, accuracy monitoring metrics.
