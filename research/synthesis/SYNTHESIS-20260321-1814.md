## 1. New Research Added This Cycle

### 1.1 Peru Contador Ecosystem — The Missing GTM Piece (market/13)

**This is the most strategically significant GTM finding since the research program began.**

Peru's micro-enterprise ecosystem is structurally dependent on external accountants (contadores). Unlike developed markets, Peruvian MYPEs overwhelmingly outsource their entire accounting and tax compliance to independent contadores. Key findings:

- **The contador is the single most trusted business advisor** most micro-enterprise owners have
- **Outsourcing pricing is standardized:** S/150-250/month ($40-67) for basic MYPE accounting
- **WhatsApp is already the primary channel** between MYPEs and their contadores — firms actively list WhatsApp numbers for client communication
- **One contador serves 30-50 MYPE clients** → converting 100 contadores = reaching 3,000-5,000 end users
- **Estimated 46,000-77,000 contadores** serve Peru's 2.3M formal MYPEs
- **The MYPE accounting outsourcing market is ~$1.5-1.9 billion/year**
- **96.6% of 384 surveyed Lima accountants** favor electronic systems (Zenodo/UCV academic study)

**Contador Partner Model:**
- Tier 1: Free dashboard showing client financial data (reduces time-per-client by 30-50%)
- Tier 2: Revenue share referral program (15-25% of subscription)
- Tier 3: Premium tools (S/50-100/month): SIRE automation, multi-client dashboard, AI anomaly alerts

**Critical product design insight:** Yaya doesn't replace the contador — it replaces the *worst part* of the contador's workflow (data collection and organization) while strengthening the *most valuable part* (trusted advisory relationship). The contador shifts from data entry to strategic advice.

**SIRE Timing:** SUNAT's Integrated Electronic Records System became mandatory for large taxpayers in January 2026 and is progressively expanding. As it reaches MYPEs, contadores will need tools that auto-format client data for SIRE submission — exactly what Yaya provides.

### 1.2 WhatsApp Flows + Voice Notes Technical Architecture (technology/12)

Complete technical blueprint for Yaya's hybrid interface architecture:

**The Hybrid Pattern:** Voice notes handle natural input ("Vendí 50 soles a María"). WhatsApp Flows handle structured confirmation (tap to confirm amount, select payment method). This eliminates the false choice between conversational AI and structured forms.

**WhatsApp Flows proven results in similar markets:**
- Consórcio Magalu (Brazil): 2.9x conversion rate increase
- Lenovo Indonesia: 8.2x higher conversion vs. website
- Farmacias del Ahorro (Mexico): 2.6x increase via WhatsApp
- Sefamerve (Turkey): 158% increase in overall conversions

**Architecture:**
- FastAPI webhook on d.yaya.sh (returns 200 in <5s, processes async)
- Celery task queue with Redis backend
- Whisper large-v3 on c.yaya.sh for STT (~500-800ms for 10s audio)
- Qwen3.5-27B on c.yaya.sh for NLU (~300-500ms)
- PostgreSQL for transactions, Redis for conversation state (24h TTL)
- **Total voice-to-response latency: ~1,300-1,800ms** — effectively instant

**Five Core WhatsApp Flows designed:**
1. Sale Confirmation (post-voice-note)
2. Daily Summary (end-of-day report)
3. Fío Registration (credit tracking)
4. Client Registration
5. Onboarding (< 3 minutes to first recorded transaction)

**Scaling analysis:** Current infrastructure handles up to 5,000 users. Whisper on 2× RTX A5000 processes ~120-240 voice notes/minute. No bottleneck until ~12,000-24,000 voice messages/day.
