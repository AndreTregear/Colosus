## 1. New Research Added This Cycle

### 1.1 Peru Payment Integration Landscape (technology/08)

Complete mapping of Peru's payment ecosystem for Yaya's Phase 1. Key findings:

- **Yape dominates with 16M+ users** (plus 3M in Bolivia = 18M total); Plin has 10M+ — both lack public developer APIs
- **Culqi is the recommended first gateway:** Native Peruvian (Credicorp/BCP group), zero setup fees, excellent REST API with Python/Node SDKs, PCI DSS certified, 3.44% + S/0.20 + IGV commission
- **Payment links via WhatsApp are the killer feature no competitor offers:** Culqi's API can generate payment links sent directly in WhatsApp for appointment deposits
- **Screenshot OCR is a viable bridge for Yape/Plin verification:** No API needed; AI reads payment confirmation screenshots to verify deposits — handles "pantallazos falsos" risk with pattern matching
- **QR interoperability advancing:** BCR mandating single QR for Yape/Plin/bank apps — when complete, Yaya generates one QR per merchant
- **Payment processing revenue potential:** At 500 salons with 30% volume capture, S/7,500-36,000/month — potentially exceeding subscription revenue
- **65% of Peruvians now banked** (Arellano 2024); digital payment adoption is no longer a barrier
- **Phased approach:** Phase 1 = Culqi links + OCR verification → Phase 2 = direct Yape QR → Phase 3 = full payment facilitation

### 1.2 WhatsApp Business API 2026 Policy & Pricing Analysis (strategy/07)

Critical update that materially reduces Yaya's #1 risk. Key findings:

- **Meta's AI agent ban (Jan 15, 2026) HELPS Yaya:** General-purpose AI chatbots are banned, but purpose-built business tools (appointments, invoicing, inventory) are explicitly allowed. Yaya is in the "compliant" category. Ban eliminates a class of potential competitors.
- **Per-message pricing revolution (July 2025):** Service messages (customer-initiated) are now **completely free**. Utility messages are free within 24h service window. Only marketing messages cost money.
- **Estimated WhatsApp API cost per Yaya user: $0.52-3.02/month** — at $13 ARPU, this maintains 77-96% gross margin on messaging costs alone
- **72-hour free entry point:** Click-to-WhatsApp ads from Instagram/Facebook make ALL messages free for 72 hours — including onboarding flows. Customer acquisition via CTWA ads costs $0 in WhatsApp fees.
- **Official API is now the right choice:** Cost structure is favorable enough ($0.52-3.02/user/month) that ban risk from unofficial APIs isn't worth it. Go official from Day 1.
- **EU forced Meta to allow rival AI chatbots (March 5, 2026)** but with per-message fees of €0.049-0.1323 that rivals call prohibitive. Brazil also forced openness. LATAM regulators trending toward EU precedent.
- **Platform dependency risk score revised: from 25/25 (Existential) to 16/25 (Critical but manageable)**
