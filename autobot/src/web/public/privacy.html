Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

At Yaya ("we", "us", "our"), we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our WhatsApp commerce automation platform and related services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, and business name when you register.
  • Business Data: Product catalog, pricing, stock levels, and business settings you configure.
  • Customer Interactions: Messages exchanged through WhatsApp that are processed by our AI system to fulfill orders and manage conversations.
  • Payment Information: Payment references and transaction data related to Yape and other payment methods.
  • Contact Form Submissions: Name, phone number, business name, and messages submitted through our website.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve our platform and services.
  • Process and manage orders on your behalf through WhatsApp.
  • Respond to your comments, questions, and customer support requests.
  • Generate business analytics and insights for your dashboard.
  • Send you technical notices, updates, and administrative messages.
  • Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activities.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on secure servers with encryption at rest and in transit. We implement industry-standard security measures including:

  • TLS encryption for all data transmission.
  • Database encryption and access controls.
  • Regular security audits and monitoring.
  • Tenant isolation — your data is completely separated from other businesses on our platform.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • With AI service providers to process conversations (data is not retained by these providers for training).
  • When required by law, regulation, or legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Yaya, our users, or the public.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information at any time.
  • Export your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Opt out of marketing communications.
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data.

6. WhatsApp Data

Messages processed through our platform are stored securely and used solely for the purpose of providing our service (order management, customer interaction, etc.). We do not use your WhatsApp messages for advertising or sell them to third parties. Conversation history is retained for service continuity and can be deleted upon request.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

Our website uses localStorage to save theme preferences and form submissions. We do not use tracking cookies or third-party analytics tools that track individual users.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
